Why Choose Us?

Bunyonyi Heights Inn. Serenity redefined

Bunyonyi heights Inn offers a serene environment for both visitors and residents of Kabale. It is located on the road towards the deepest lake in Uganda called Lake Bunyonyi.

We offer accomodation at very affordable rates in the most comfortable environment. Our exquisite local and foreign meals are cooked by our very experienced chef.

Furnished apartments/Boutique hotel & Accom. With a perfect balance of serenity luxury &privacy on the main Rd to L.Bunyonyi & ideal for budget travelers
